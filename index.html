Program Objectives

What you will learn through this program

Learning Objectives
  • What foods to eat to make sure you recover quickly from soreness and injuries, and perform better
  • How to meal prep and integrate healthy eating as a lifestyle
  • Portion control and timing of meals (pre and post workout)
  • How to have proper form of exercises to reap the most benefits and prevent injury
  • How to properly warm up and stretch before and after a workout

Program Details

What you will get from this program
  • A lifelong transformation
  • A detailed, easy to follow grocery list
  • A 4-week “meal plan” with timing for pre/post workout
  • A 4-week workout plan with videos of each workout session or major movement. Program can be completed more than once.
  • A community forum via private Facebook page
  • 1 weekly “check-in” via call or Facetime

Program Expectations

  • 100% effort

  • Check in (once a week)
  • Completion of program
